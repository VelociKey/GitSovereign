# K109: Strategic Horizon (24-Month Roadmap) - Project GitSovereign

The 24-month horizon for GitSovereign maps the evolution from a developer tool to a mission-critical institutional infrastructure.

## Phase 1: Firehorse Foundation (Month 0-6)
- **Objective**: Establish the "Firehorse" (Sub-Second Recovery) and "Identity Shield" (Firebase/SPIFFE) as the industry benchmark for data sovereignty.
- **Milestones**:
    - Launch of the Single-Binary Firehorse agent for local workstations.
    - Integration of Google Drive/GCS (via MCP) for cloud-backed sovereignty.
    - Implementation of the **Pinnacle-Assurance** reporting engine.

## Phase 2: Sovereign Intelligence (Month 7-18)
- **Objective**: Build the **Sovereign Search Index** across all harvested repositories.
- **Milestones**:
    - Federated search across thousands of repositories with zero-disk metadata extraction.
    - Automated "Chaos Rehydration" simulations to verify recovery integrity.
    - Integration with enterprise PBAC (Google Workspace Groups).

## Phase 3: Institutional Sovereign Hub (Month 19-24)
- **Objective**: Final transition to the **Institutional Sovereign Hub** (multi-organization federated backup).
- **Milestones**:
    - Transition to the **Federated Storage Mesh** for sub-second recovery at petabyte scale.
    - Automated SOC2/HIPAA compliance audit trail generation.
    - Cross-cloud disaster recovery orchestration between major providers (GCP/AWS/Azure).
